## Soul-check (the headline)

> *"The soul still coheres — and notably, it gets STRONGER under this review rather than being dismantled.
> Pure flooding, anonymity-via-uniformity, sealed-sender, absolute-TTL self-destruct, no-server/OOB identity,
> and gathering-scope are all the right calls; every one of the strongest alternatives preserves all seven
> invariants, and several make the invariants MORE robust than v0.2.*
>
> *Two things to face squarely. FIRST, the single biggest UNSOLVED thing is not a crypto gap — it is that the
> two ranked-highest goals are in direct physical tension: 'works in a total blackout' and 'pure flooding for
> anonymity' collide at the PERCOLATION CLIFF, where below a critical mean degree the network shatters into
> islands that never exchange a blob; the only honest fixes (blind human ferrying, opportunistic longer-range
> radios) are probabilistic, not a guarantee. SECOND, even with every fix, the persistent wide-coverage
> sensor-net + PHY-fingerprinting adversary is not defeated, only made more expensive — device-linkage should
> be assumed ≈1.0, and the most defensible promise is 'an attacker cannot deny service to or deanonymize
> friend-to-friend traffic in a dense gathering without physically deploying O(crowd-size) radios and sensors.'
> That is a real, falsifiable, honorable guarantee — but narrower than the marketing instinct wants, and
> stating it plainly (in the app, not just the doc) is the act that keeps the soul intact."*

## Best alternatives (creative, soul-preserving; ranked by priority)

### Adopt now
1. **Rateless set reconciliation (Erlay-style IBLT/minisketch)** — neighbors exchange a compact sketch of their ID *sets* and transfer only the symmetric difference (cost ∝ difference). Fixes the airtime wall, trial-decrypt cost, battery, and gives anti-abuse a fast-drop hook — all invariants preserved. *(Bitcoin-proven.)*
2. **Receive-before-originate gate + phone-local Poisson (Loopix) outbound mixing + self-loops** — never originate a fresh real ID "cold"; bury it among relayed novelty; Poisson timing reveals nothing; self-loops double as cover + active-attack detection + a no-routing reachability sensor. Defeats first-emergence provenance + timing tells.
3. **Link-local relay credentials spent INTO the link** — meter *relaying per neighbor per session* with single-use tokens consumed in the BLE handshake (never in the blob). No tree, no global clock, no convergence, no on-wire identity → solves the #1 open problem within the invariants.
4. **Truth-in-labeling UX + mutual-presence pairing (mutual QR + emoji SAS) + honest send/trust states** — drop "leave no durable trace"; honest bottle metaphor; "Released to the soup"/"Holding"/"Expired" (never false "delivered"); `Verified/Unverified/Key-changed!` trust states. Closes the two most likely "gets-a-user-arrested" harms for the price of copy + screens.

### Soon
5. **Per-blob VDF (sequential time-lock) replacing PoW** — sequentiality collapses the 10⁴–10⁸× mains-power asymmetry to a small constant; iteration count is a *trustless wall-clock age-floor* (structurally kills clock-skew immortality). Class-group params → no trusted setup. Verify cheap; generation is the equalizer.
6. **Crypto-shred-on-read via puncturable encryption + decouple delete-token from message-ID** — one long-lived pubkey (great for QR); reading punctures the key (FS default, no server, no prekey exhaustion); "disappear-after-read" = local puncture with zero wire signal. `delete-token = H(domain_delete‖secret)` decoupled from `message-ID` → purge without proving the blob was real, and mint decoy deletes for cover (breaks the structural wall).
7. **Hop-energy decrement (safe dual of reset-on-hop) + gossip-median mesh clock** — a decrement-only energy scalar (can only shorten life ⇒ safe unauthenticated) as a clock-independent death bound; passive trimmed-median creation-ts estimate flags clock-untrusted phones.
8. **Sliding-window/aging seen-record (cuckoo+timestamp / two-generation A2)** replacing the monolithic 30-day Bloom — window provably ≥ maxTTL+margin even under flood (FIFO-by-time, not FP-by-saturation); supports exact deletion for panic-wipe.
9. **Density-adaptive auto-knobs** — cover floor, relay floor, token price all keyed to locally-observed distinct *peer radios*; dense ⇒ throttle, near-cliff ⇒ be generous. Resolves the relay tragedy-of-the-commons + cold-start without identity.
10. **Opportunistic blind ferrying + Wi-Fi Aware/LoRa carriers** — mobility-aware retention ("am I in a stale pocket?", no position/destination) + the identical uniform soup over NAN (~100 m, dodges iOS throttle) / optional LoRa (km, low-rate). Offline accelerators the BLE-only core never depends on.

### Research
11. **Fountain/rateless K-of-N shards** (anti-suppression, anti-eviction-steering, graceful degradation past the scale cliff) — but multiplies the airtime wall, so small N for high-stakes messages only.

## Cross-cutting insights

1. **Five "separate" open problems share ONE root cause:** the wire carries no state a node can reason about except "this is a blob." The high-leverage move is to add **one class of identity-free, blob-or-neighborhood-scoped signal** (reconciliation sketches, link tokens, VDF age, density observation) that feeds all five — never an endpoint-correlated signal.
2. **Deepest tension = anonymity-via-uniformity vs every discrimination the system needs.** Resolution: move discrimination **off the wire into link-local, ephemeral, per-session decisions** (token spend in the handshake, sketch decode, per-session ranking) that leave no durable per-peer state and no on-wire metadata.
3. **Per-blob uniformity ≠ what users think.** The honest, measurable claim is **origination-event K-anonymity** (K = cover-originations / real-originations), surfaced in-app as a charge-independent floor. Three findings converge on this — the single most important conceptual correction.
4. **Assume device-linkage ≈ 1.0.** PHY fingerprinting survives MAC/payload rotation, so every higher-layer defense must be safe even when the transmitter is uniquely labeled — which forces the K-anonymity framing. Run a USRP self-audit and publish the number.
5. **Optional active-delete spends irreversible anonymity budget for a guarantee it can't deliver.** Change what "delete" *is*: crypto-shred-on-read (zero wire signal) for the common case; decoupled delete-token for the rare global case; default OFF, labeled "emits a detectable signal."
6. **Generalize the §9 internet-bridge philosophy into a design rule:** every capability that bends an invariant must be an opt-in accelerator the BLE-only/offline/uniform core never depends on (governs NAN/LoRa, the standby cover floor, active-delete).
7. **Honesty is the product's strongest asset AND biggest unshipped risk** — honesty in a doc ≠ honesty in a running app; truth-in-labeling UX converts limitations into trust and pre-empts the researcher-dunk cycle.
8. **The bootstrap problem is go-to-market, not physics** — the adoption unit is a *planned gathering* (organizer kits, pair-to-activate at N=2, standby at N=0), not metropolis virality.
9. **The fixes COMPOSE into a stack** — reconciliation carries the link tokens and surfaces the newcomer delta that eviction protects; Poisson mixing provides the cover the density controller tunes and whose self-loops feed the reachability sensor; VDF backstops the hop-energy+clock; crypto-shred removes the wire-delete substrate the anonymity attacks feast on.
10. **The hardest residual:** a true per-sender quota is fundamentally incompatible with invariants 3 & 4. The achievable shadow is "sustained rate made physically expensive (VDF) + locally bounded (link tokens + per-neighbor caps)." Stop hoping for a quota; design to the shadow. (RLN-on-the-social-graph is the only true quota and bends invariants 1/3/6 → research-only.)

## Recommended roadmap

- **P0 — Re-scope & measure.** Airtime-budget table beside the storage table; reframe public claims (airtime wall, K-anonymity, honest bottle promise).
- **P1 — Highest-ROI wins.** Rateless set reconciliation; mutual-presence pairing + truth-in-labeling UX + honest send/trust states.
- **P2 — Anti-flood + anonymity primitives.** Link-local relay tokens; Poisson mixing + receive-before-originate gate + self-loops.
- **P3 — Lifetime/storage hardening.** Hop-energy + gossip-median clock; sliding-window seen-record; youngest-by-real-age eviction; density auto-knobs.
- **P4 — Percolation + cold-start.** Blind ferrying; opportunistic NAN/LoRa; organizer gathering-kits + pair-to-activate + standby mode.
- **P5 — Serverless key-management.** Puncturable-encryption crypto-shred; decoupled delete-token; X-Wing hybrid + key-committing AEAD; deniable authenticator.
- **P6 — Continuous verification.** Multi-OS transport conformance harness; adversarial-eviction CI gate; USRP PHY self-audit; internet-disabled CI.
